Frequently Asked Questions

Why does my power shower make a buzzing sound?
If your power shower emits a buzzing or clicking sound, it might be due to debris blocking the pump or filters. Cleaning the internal components can often resolve this.
What does a shower cartridge do?
A shower cartridge controls both water flow and temperature, ensuring smooth operation when turning the handle or lever.
Is plumber's putty safe for installing shower heads?
No, plumber's putty can damage plastic components in shower systems. Use thread seal tape (Teflon tape) instead for a secure and safe seal.
Should my mirror be the same width as my vanity unit?
It's best to choose a mirror slightly narrower than your vanity to maintain balance and avoid overwhelming the space.
What exactly is a bathroom vanity unit?
A vanity unit combines a sink and storage, helping conceal plumbing and reduce clutter in your bathroom.
What types of vanity units are available?
Options include freestanding, wall-mounted, corner, and combined basin-storage vanity units, each suited to different bathroom layouts.
When is the best time to bathe toddlers?
Bathing toddlers after teatime helps them unwind before bed. It also creates a comforting routine that can aid sleep.
