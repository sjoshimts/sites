About Us

Empowering Safety, Protecting Lives.

At Firechief®, we envision a world where people can live and work free from the fear of fire. Since developing our first fire blanket in 1963, we have been resolutely committed to making the world a safer place.

We provide a range of proven fire safety products trusted to perform when the heat is on, all underpinned by an exceptional end-to-end customer experience.

Operating from our Head Office in Rutland and Global Distribution Centre in Corby, we work with an extensive network of experts and hold stock of up to half a million products, ready for immediate dispatch.
