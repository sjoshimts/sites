The Beginning

Our Heritage

A Legacy Born in the Apennine Mountains

Carnevale dates back to 1966 when two young brothers, Carmine and Giovanni Carnevale, journeyed from the village of Capracotta in Molise, Southern Italy. With a passion for authentic flavors, they established the company, manufacturing and selling mozzarella and ricotta for the first time in central London.

Today, Carnevale remains a fiercely proud family-run business. The second generation has joined to continue the work of evolving the company, upholding its reputation as an industry-leading manufacturer, importer, and distributor of premium foods, provisions, and beverages.
